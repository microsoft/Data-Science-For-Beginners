## Write Data Ethics Case Study

## Instructions

You don learn about different [Data Ethics Wahala](README.md#2-ethics-challenges) and see example of [Case Studies](README.md#3-case-studies) wey show data ethics wahala for real-life situations.

For dis assignment, you go write your own case study wey show one data ethics wahala wey you don experience, or wey dey happen for real-life situation wey you sabi. Just follow dis steps:

1. `Choose Data Ethics Wahala`. Check [the lesson examples](README.md#2-ethics-challenges) or look for online examples like [the Deon Checklist](https://deon.drivendata.org/examples/) to get idea.

2. `Talk About Real-Life Example`. Think about one situation wey you don hear about (news, research study etc.) or wey you don experience (local community), wey dis wahala happen. Think about the data ethics questions wey dey related to the wahala - and talk about the bad things or wahala wey fit happen because of dis issue. Bonus points: think about solution or process wey fit help stop or reduce the bad impact of dis wahala.

3. `Add Related Resources`. Share one or more resources (links to article, personal blog post or image, online research paper etc.) to show say dis thing really happen for real life. Bonus points: share resources wey also show the bad things & wahala wey come from the incident, or show good steps wey dem take to stop am from happening again.
