# Soda Profits

## Instructions

Di [Coca Cola Co spreadsheet](../../../../2-Working-With-Data/06-non-relational/CocaColaCo.xlsx) dey miss some calculations. Wetin you go do na: 

1. Calculate di Gross profits for FY '15, '16, '17, and '18
    - Gross Profit = Net Operating revenues - Cost of goods sold
1. Calculate di average of all di gross profits. Try make you use function do am.
    - Average = Sum of gross profits divided by di number of fiscal years (10)
    - Documentation for di [AVERAGE function](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/office/average-function-047bac88-d466-426c-a32b-8f33eb960cf6)
1. Dis na Excel file, but e fit edit am for any spreadsheet platform
