# Check for ansa

Dis one na continuation of di [assignment](../14-Introduction/assignment.md) wey dey di previous lesson, wey we small small check di data set. Now we go look di data well well.

Di question wey di client wan make we ansa na: **Yellow taxi passengers for New York City dey tip drivers pass for winter or summer?**

Una team dey di [Analyzing](README.md) stage for di Data Science Lifecycle, wey una go do exploratory data analysis for di dataset. Dem don give una one notebook and dataset wey get 200 taxi transactions from January and July 2019.

## Wetin to do

For dis folder, you go see one [notebook](assignment.ipynb) and data from di [Taxi & Limousine Commission](https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/open-datasets/dataset-taxi-yellow?tabs=azureml-opendatasets). Check di [dataset's dictionary](https://www1.nyc.gov/assets/tlc/downloads/pdf/data_dictionary_trip_records_yellow.pdf) and [user guide](https://www1.nyc.gov/assets/tlc/downloads/pdf/trip_record_user_guide.pdf) to sabi more about di data.

Use di techniques wey dey dis lesson to do your own EDA for di notebook (you fit add cells if you wan) and ansa di questions wey dey below:

- Wetin else for di data fit affect di tip amount?
- Which columns no go dey useful to ansa di client question?
- From wetin dem don give so far, di data dey show any evidence say people dey tip different for winter and summer?
