# Tell story

## Instructions

Data Science na di work wey dey about storytelling. Choose any dataset and write small paper about di story wey you fit tell about am. Wetin you dey hope say your dataset go show? Wetin you go do if di things wey e show dey cause wahala? Wetin you go do if di data no gree show di secret wey e get? Think about di different things wey fit happen with your dataset and write dem down.

## Rubric

Exemplary | Adequate | Needs Improvement
--- | --- | -- |

One-page essay dey for .doc format, di dataset dey explain well, e get document, e get credit, and di story wey dem talk about am dey make sense with plenty example from di data.| Short essay dey but e no get plenty detail | Di essay no get one of di things wey dem mention above.
